I am a member of technical staff at World Labs. My research interests are in computer vision and graphics, focusing on 2D and 3D generative models.

I received my Ph.D. at the University of Washington in 2024, co-advised by Ira Kemelmacher-Shlizerman, Steven Seitz and Brian Curless. I was a student researcher at Google from 2022 to 2024, during which my virtual try-on projects were published and launched as products. Before that, I obtained my B.S. degree at Peking University in 2018, advised by Prof. Yizhou Wang.
